About Me

I like troubleshooting computer issues so I enjoy my work for the most part I also love nature and looking for bugs. That is why I decided I will work with code and possibly get into developing positions at my work. If you are curious, I have seen snow before I came to Minnesota. Starting from "Have you tried turning it on and off" to "What server are you trying to connect to?" I starting liking coding when I saw the show Mr Robot. Although he is not a programmer per se, but there are some things shown in that show that "trigger that portion of my brain."
